Workshop #1  ·  Registration open

Cutting-edge Alternative Models & Essential Skills for Advanced R&I

29 Sept – 02 Oct 2026

Dear colleagues, dear PhD students and researchers of the FOCUS-4R consortium,

We are delighted to announce the first workshop of the FOCUS-4R project, Workshop #1, hosted by i3S – Universidade do Porto (29 September – 02 October 2026, hybrid format).

Objectives

To strengthen capacities in advanced New Approach Methodologies (NAMs) and to equip participants with essential non-scientific skills for high-quality, responsible and innovative research.

Main topics

Topic 1

Advanced Cell Models & HTS

Spheroids, organoids, organ-on-chip, CAM assays, HTS/HCS, bioimaging, mechanobiology.

Topic 2

GLP & HRS4R

Good Laboratory Practices and the HRS4R framework for research quality and integrity.

Programme — 3 phases

Phase I · early September 2026 · online

Theoretical foundations

Online sessions introducing the NAMs and core concepts ahead of the practical week.

Phase II · 29 Sept – 02 Oct 2026 · Porto

Intensive hands-on training

Four days of on-site practical work at i3S — advanced cell culture, imaging, HTS/HCS.

Phase III · October 2026 · online

GLP implementation sessions

Led by C.RIS Pharma — implementing Good Laboratory Practices in your institution.
