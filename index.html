Dear colleagues, dear PhD students and researchers of the FOCUS-4R consortium,

We are delighted to announce the first workshop of the FOCUS-4R project, Workshop #1, hosted by i3S – Universidade do Porto (29 September – 02 October 2026, hybrid format).

Objectives

To strengthen capacities in advanced New Approach Methodologies (NAMs) and to equip participants with essential non-scientific skills for high-quality, responsible and innovative research.

Main topics

Topic 1 — Advanced Cell Models & HTS

Spheroids, organoids, organ-on-chip, CAM assays, HTS/HCS, bioimaging, mechanobiology.
Topic 2 — GLP & HRS4R

Good Laboratory Practices and the HRS4R framework.

Programme — 3 phases

▸ Phase I · early September 2026 (online) — Theoretical foundations
▸ Phase II · 29 Sept – 02 Oct 2026 (Porto) — Intensive hands-on training
▸ Phase III · October 2026 (online) — GLP implementation sessions, led by C.RIS Pharma
